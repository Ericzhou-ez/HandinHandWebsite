PARTNERS AND SPONSORS

We are honored to be supported by our esteemed partners and sponsors, who are true leaders and innovators in their respective fields. Their world-class expertise and invaluable opportunities profoundly benefit our members and our mission.

BL Education Consulting is a world-renowned educational institution that aids students in preparing for university. They provide us with not only valuable resources and guidance from their counselors, but also financially support us.

Code Connect is a non-profit based in Vancouver and their mission is to bridge the digital divide, allowing all students access to coding education. We have partnered with them to provide peer tutoring.

Mood Psych UBC partnered with us to conduct seminars focused on the destigmatization of mental health issues. Additionally, they provide an anonymous Chinese disscussion forum, creating a safe space for international students studying abroad.

Covenant House Vancouver is an organization supporting homeless teenagers and addressing their mental health issues. We have proudly donated over $2000 to their cause and had the privilege of assisting them in writing messages to their donors.
