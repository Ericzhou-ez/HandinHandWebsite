Values

The compassion and empathy we share at HIH drive everything we do. We're committed to making the world a better place by supporting mental well-being and we envision a world liberated from mental health stigma, where understanding goes hand in hand with empathy.

All in all, Hand in Hand volunteers have dedicated over 1400 hours to building this vibrant community.

Our community members contribute uniquely, driven by the fervent passion of uplifting others. Explore our initiatives and you will see diverse efforts united by a common goal:
