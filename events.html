Hand in Hand

Events:

Peer Tutoring (SFPT)

The Hand in Hand Stress-Free Peer Tutoring (SFPT) program empowers 100 underprivileged students across Vancouver each year by imparting more than 200 hours of free education.

HIH Forum

The Hand in Hand Poetry Forum provides an anonymous platform for students to express their emotions and find positive coping mechanisms for academic stress. This safe and supportive space fosters a healthy community, reminiscent of a sharing circle.

Fundraisers

Hand in Hand conducts two impactful seminars per annum aimed at promoting the destigmatization of mental health issues. These sessions provide valuable insights and foster open conversations regarding mental health challenges.

Vast Online Presence

Hand in Hand has built a loyal and engaging community of over 5 000 Instagram followers. Through regular interactions and Instagram stories, we maintain an active dialogue and support network.
